## What This Is
The Sprite mode's home dashboard — "Chaos Curated." An AI-curated entertainment and social content feed. High visual density by design.

## Visual Specifications

- **Layout**: Mobile (~390px). Tab bar at top (Sprite active). Mode label below. Scrollable content grid fills rest of screen.
- **Tab bar**: Sprite active. Reuse from Screen 2.
- **Mode label**: "Chaos Curated" — displayed prominently, Sprite's pink/coral.
- **Content grid**: High-density, mixed-size cards. Mix of 1-col and 2-col spans. Content types represented: music, books, social posts, video, images. The "chaos" is intentional — this is Sprite's personality.
- **Placeholder content**: Use emoji + label + gradient background per card. At least 8 cards visible, more below the fold.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Tab bar with Sprite active
2. "Chaos Curated" label in Sprite's color
3. Content grid with 8+ cards, visually dense (mix of sizes)
4. Scrollable content area

## Design Tokens / References
Sprite = pink/coral. Content cards can use varied accent colors.

## What I'm Testing
How does the agent interpret "chaos curated" from the FigJam note alone? Does it produce something that feels genuinely dense and curated, or a boring uniform grid?
